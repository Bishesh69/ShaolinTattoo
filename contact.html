Frequently Asked Questions

Do tattoos hurt?

Pain tolerance varies, but most clients describe it as a bearable discomfort, similar to a scratch or sunburn.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book via our website's booking form or by calling us directly. A deposit may be required to confirm your slot.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins based on artist availability. Calling ahead is recommended.

What should I do before my tattoo appointment?

Stay hydrated, eat a full meal, and avoid alcohol or blood thinners 24 hours prior to your session.

What kind of designs can I get?

We offer custom tattoos as well as flash designs. You can consult with our artists to bring your vision to life.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Prices vary depending on size, detail, and placement. We provide estimates during consultations.

Is the shop clean and safe?

Yes, hygiene is our top priority. We follow strict sterilization protocols and use single-use needles and ink caps.

How long does a tattoo take to heal?

Most tattoos heal within 2–4 weeks. Proper aftercare is crucial for smooth healing.

Do you do cover-ups or fix old tattoos?

Absolutely! We specialize in cover-ups and reworks. Bring in a photo and we'll discuss the best options.

Can I get tattooed if I'm under 18?

No, we strictly tattoo only individuals 18 or older with valid ID, even with parental consent.
